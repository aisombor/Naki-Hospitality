About

Naki Group

Who We Are

Rooted from Cambodia, the Pearl of Asia, Naki Group has grown from a local real estate and property management firm into an investment firm focused on developing the Indo-China belt through sustainable investments into property, property related as well as service based businesses.

Our team includes dedicated professionals who truly cares about the markets that we influence. Naki has grown significantly over the decade and aims to be the one-stop-shop for business and investment opportunities within Cambodia and the Indo-China belt. We bring a wealth of experience into the market to create modern and innovative solutions to the real estate market.

At our core, Naki Group is a dynamic team that holds the integrity of our people as a core value.

We treasure the relationships nurtured with our clients and working partners and we take pride in our ability to create economically sound investments and proprietary opportunities for our partners and investors.

We respect heritage. We envision the future.
